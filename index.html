Five Nights at Epstein's

How To Play

You are stationary in your hideout. Monitor 11 cameras across the island, use audio lures to redirect enemies, and survive until dawn.

Controls

  • Mouse: Select cameras and survey each area
  • Control Panel: Restart broken cameras or close/open air vents
  • Audio Lure: Activate on the camera interface to draw enemies away

Nights

Night 1 — Epstein

Starts at Camera 11. Constantly play sounds near his position to lure him away. Overusing audio on one camera breaks it — fix it immediately via the Control Panel.

Night 2 — Trump

Attacks through vents connected to Camera 1 and 2. Hear banging? Close the vents. When banging stops, reopen them or you suffocate. Always prioritize Epstein first.

Night 3 — Stephen Hawking

Stays at Camera 6 and is immune to audio lures. Electrocute him periodically to keep him in place — if ignored, he heads straight for you.

Night 4 & 5 — All Three

All enemies active simultaneously with increased speed. System failures are constant. A single second of delay can end your run.

Night 6 — Epstein Encore

Epstein becomes completely unpredictable — can appear anywhere, at any time. No patterns. Pure chaos.

Custom Night

Set each enemy's difficulty individually. Mix any combination including the secret Golden Hawking variant.
